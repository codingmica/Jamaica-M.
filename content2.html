Personal Growth

Throughout my educational journey, I have encountered numerous challenges that have shaped my resilience, adaptability, and commitment to lifelong learning.

In Senior High School, where I initially faced difficulty due to a lack of experience with research, I learned the importance of determination and seeking guidance.

Despite setbacks, such as not winning the research competition, the experience developed my confidence and public speaking skills, preparing me to embrace challenges with a positive mindset.

One pivotal moment occurred during my pre-defense presentation when I found myself presenting alone. With a room filled with attentive peers and teachers, I had to overcome self-doubt and deliver the presentation solo.

Through this experience, I not only grew academically but also developed greater self-confidence and the ability to stay composed under pressure.

Transitioning to college and choosing to study Information Technology came with new and unexpected challenges. Initially, I struggled to adapt and found myself questioning my choice of study.

However, with dedication and self-reflection, I began to develop a genuine appreciation for the field and the problem-solving skills it fosters.

This realization has guided my approach to learning: embracing perseverance and nurturing a mindset that seeks growth through every experience.

Each experience has contributed to a deeper understanding of my potential, building a foundation of confidence, resilience, and a commitment to excel in any field I pursue.

I remain dedicated to learning, not just to reach academic goals but to continuously evolve, adapt, and contribute meaningfully in my personal and professional life.
